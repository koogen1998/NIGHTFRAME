About Frame Cinema


Since 2017

Frame Cinema specialising in quality mainstream and Arthouse cinema, is just 5 minutes away from Bukit jalil. We believe in the complete 'cinematic experience'- big screens, beautifully appointed seating and a warm, friendly environment. And what's more, you won't need to pay for parking.

Our graceful foyer will entice you to share a drink with a friend before viewing our selection of local and international feature films. Before entering our spacious cinemas, ensure you have chosen from our beautifully handmade choc top selection. Fancy something savory? Why not taste our delicious freshly popped popcorn included in all of our specially selected combos.

At Classic Cinema, our eclectic mix of films means every taste is catered for; from big Hollywood blockbusters to the best in foreign cinema, kids' flicks to family favorites, Classic Cinema is your perfect cinema destination.
